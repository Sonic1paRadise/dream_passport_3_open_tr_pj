Sega of Europe have confirmed the stunning line up of games to be available for Dreamcast at the launch of the new 128-bit super-console. September 23rd, Dreamcast launch day will see 10 titles available as promised. This is the strongest software launch line up of any console ever. The next month will see a further 15 titles released both by Sega and the large community of 3rd party developers supporting the platform.
